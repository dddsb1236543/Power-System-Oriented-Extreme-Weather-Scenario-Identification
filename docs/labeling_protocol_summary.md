\# Labeling Protocol Summary



This document summarizes the power-system-oriented extreme-weather labeling protocol used in the manuscript.



The labeling process is designed to identify weather scenarios that are not only meteorologically abnormal but also relevant to power-system operation risk.



A sample is labeled as a power-system-oriented extreme-weather scenario when meteorological abnormality is jointly supported by power-system response or external event evidence.



\## General Labeling Logic



The labeling process considers four types of information:



1\. Meteorological abnormality;

2\. Power-system response;

3\. External event evidence;

4\. Manual verification.
